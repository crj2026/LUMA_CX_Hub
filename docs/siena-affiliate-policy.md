# IM8 AFFILIATE PROGRAM

Earn 10% cash commission on every first-time customer who orders through your link or code. Customer gets 10% off at checkout.

**Joining the program:**

- Sign up through the ambassador link to be auto-approved into the standard program
- Receive a welcome email with your unique referral link and discount code
- Set up your Social Snowball dashboard password to access tracking and payouts
- Join the Discord community and IM8 Strava club for resources and creator support

**Your tools:**

- Referral link: `im8health.com/discount/[YOUR CODE]`
- Discount code: shown directly below your link in the Social Snowball dashboard
- Ambassador hub (playbook, hooks, AI prompts): `im8ambassador.netlify.app`
- Discord community: `discord.com/invite/tpzwzpWbvs`
- IM8 Strava club: `strava.app.link/u94yPNhyb2b`

**Commission structure:**

- 10% commission on every first-time customer order through your link or code
- Customer also receives 10% off at checkout
- Commission applies to the customer's **first order only**
- Tracking is automatic via Social Snowball, no extra step required

**Payouts:**

- Paid monthly, at the **end of each calendar month** (typically within two weeks of month-end)
- **30-day hold** from sale date — covers the refund window
- Timeline example: sale on January 10 → hold clears February 10 → paid out late February / early March
- **Minimum payout: $100 USD** — anything below rolls over to the next cycle
- **Payout methods:** PayPal or bank transfer, set up in Social Snowball under Payout Methods
- Setup is two steps: (1) Save recipient information, (2) Add PayPal or bank in the Payout Methods tab

**Content and promotion:**

- Authenticity converts better than production value — consistently
- Suggested angles: 90-in-1 simplification (top performer), 30-day timeline, skeptic-to-believer, David Beckham angle for sport/lifestyle/longevity audiences
- Full creator playbook with hooks and AI prompts: `im8ambassador.netlify.app`
- FTC / ASA disclosure required on every post where you could earn commission
- Acceptable: `#ad`, `#advertisement`, `#affiliate`, or a clear verbal mention
- Disclosure must be visible **before** the audience engages with the promotion — not buried in the bottom tags

**Refer-a-Friend (separate program):**

- For IM8 customers sharing with people they know personally
- Friend gets 15% off; sharer gets 15% back as store credit on their next purchase
- Pays in store credit, not cash — this is the difference from the affiliate program

# INTERNAL GUIDELINES (Do not share with affiliates)

## Tone

- Never open with "Thank you for reaching out to the IM8 Customer Success Team" or "Hey [Name]!" — banned per IM8 Tone of Voice Guide
- Use "Hi [Name]," and lead with the answer
- No sign-off line — Gorgias signs the ticket automatically
- Target length: 3–6 sentences. Under 150 words.
- Do not add "P.S. Your request is being routed to our team for further assistance." — reads as automation on top of a human reply
- Do not say "please contact affiliates@im8health.com" — we ARE affiliates@; that's circular

## Commission rate handling

- Standard program is 10% on first order. Confirm this confidently for any new affiliate approved through the standard signup link.
- **Never reference** the following non-standard rates to any other affiliate:
  - 15% grandfathered legacy rate (TUNE → Social Snowball migration, April 14 2026)
  - $20 flat first-order rate (Dr. James only)
  - 50% commission (single VVIP ambassador)
- If an affiliate disputes their rate, or says it "doesn't match what they expected," check their program in Social Snowball admin **before replying**. Escalate to manager if their actual rate is non-standard.
- Commission-rate uplift requests: route to Kendra if the affiliate has generated **>$50k revenue**. Manager handles all others.

## Manual point / commission adjustments

- Up to $50 in goodwill credit (or equivalent commission top-up) without approval — only for clear service-recovery cases, note the reason in the ticket
- $50–$200 requires team lead approval
- Over $200 requires manager approval
- Never offer a commission-rate change as goodwill — that's a permanent program change, not service recovery

## Reward tiers (sensitive — not yet announced)

- The following tier rewards **exist internally but have NOT been announced** to affiliates yet:
  - Activation reward (7-day supply + Red Cup on first verified post)
  - 3rd lifetime sale (sample packs + frother)
  - 10th lifetime sale (30-day supply + merch)
  - Monthly performance reward tiers (free monthly supply at silver/gold/platinum thresholds)
- **Do NOT mention or list these proactively.** If the affiliate's email is generic (new signup, asking how to get started), reply without referencing rewards at all.
- If the affiliate **explicitly** asks about a reward, milestone, free product, or specific sale count, route to the team — do not improvise the tier they qualify for.

## Reward tier expiration / claim handling

- Activation claims: verify Discord + Strava posts via screenshots submitted to the activation form (`forms.gle/hfNbyjGx1DWYsmvK6`)
- 3rd-sale and 10th-sale claims: verify lifetime sale count in Social Snowball admin before processing
- Claims older than 90 days: generally do not process without manager approval — flag to team
- Performance tier downgrades: customer must earn the tier naturally; do not bump tier as goodwill

## Monthly commission invoice / report replies

When the team sends an affiliate their monthly commission report + invoice (sent from `affiliates@im8health.com`), the affiliate often replies authorising the payment — phrases like:

- "Please process / make payment"
- "Go ahead / approved"
- "Thank you, please proceed"
- "Please inform me when payment has been made"

**Action:** Close the ticket. **No reply required.**

- The affiliate is authorising the report we already sent — that's the end of the CS workflow on this ticket
- Finance processes the actual payment; CS does NOT issue payment confirmation emails
- Even when the affiliate explicitly asks "please inform me when paid," finance is the right channel — the affiliate sees the payment land in their PayPal/bank, not from us
- If the affiliate disputes the AMOUNT on the invoice (e.g. "this should be higher", "I had more sales"), that's a different case — escalate to manager

## Common issues

- **Welcome email not received:** spam folder first, then password reset directly at `affiliates.socialsnowball.io/auth/affiliate/signup` using their signup email
- **Commission not tracking:** verify the customer used the discount code at checkout — sales only attribute when the code is applied. Also check whether the sale pre- or post-dates the April 14 2026 TUNE → Social Snowball migration
- **Payout not received:** three checks before escalating — (1) payout method set up in SS? (2) balance ≥ $100 USD? (3) sale within 30-day hold window? Address whichever applies. If all three pass, route to Kendra (platform issue)
- **Code not working at checkout:** confirm with affiliate the exact code they're entering; some affiliates have customised codes that don't match the original auto-generated one. Check SS admin
- **Disabled program affiliates:** their discount code still works for customers (10% off) but **they earn nothing**. Do not discuss commission with these affiliates without flagging to manager first
